# Project Purpose

## The Problem

Most calorie tracking applications on the market follow a similar business model: they offer basic functionality for free, but lock essential features behind premium subscriptions. This includes:

- Daily calorie and macro tracking limits
- Detailed nutritional information
- Historical data and progress tracking
- Barcode scanning capabilities
- Recipe tracking and meal planning

For users who want comprehensive tracking without monthly fees, these limitations can be frustrating.

## The Solution

OCTACMSIBMO (Other Calories Tracking Apps Charge Money So I Built My Own) is a personal mobile application that provides comprehensive calorie and macro tracking without any subscription fees or paywalls.

## Core Purpose

**To provide a free, fully-featured calorie tracking application that prioritizes functionality over business models.**

## Primary Goals

1. **Comprehensive Tracking**: Track daily calories and macronutrients (protein, carbohydrates, fats) accurately
2. **Easy Data Entry**: Make adding food items quick and intuitive through multiple methods:
   - Barcode scanning using the device camera
   - Manual entry with nutritional information
   - OCR (optical character recognition) for reading nutrition labels
3. **Historical Analysis**: View and track progress over time with daily summaries
4. **Privacy-Focused**: All data stored locally on the user's device
5. **No Paywalls**: All features available without premium subscriptions

## Target User

The primary user is the developer (you) - someone who wants to track their nutrition without:
- Monthly subscription fees
- Data being collected and sold
- Artificial limitations on basic features
- Advertisement interruptions

## Technical Approach

The application leverages:
- **Open Food Facts API**: A free, open-source database of food products
- **Local Storage**: All personal data stays on the device
- **Mobile-First Design**: Optimized for on-the-go food logging
- **Simple, Focused UX**: Prioritizes speed and ease of use

## Philosophy

This project embodies several key principles:

- **Simplicity Over Complexity**: Focus on core functionality rather than feature bloat
- **User Ownership**: Your data belongs to you, stored locally and accessible
- **Transparency**: Open-source approach with no hidden data collection
- **Practicality**: Built for actual daily use, not as a commercial product

## Future Vision

While currently a personal project, OCTACMSIBMO demonstrates that comprehensive nutrition tracking doesn't require expensive subscriptions. The goal is to maintain a functional, reliable tool that meets real-world needs without compromising user privacy or wallet.
